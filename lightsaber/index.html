FiveIconDock

5 icons on your dock, no jailbreak required

It is recommended to use a test device only. These payloads were originally part of the DarkSword in-the-wild malware campaign. All C2 communication has been stripped and the source has been thoroughly reviewed for obfuscated callbacks, but there is still inherent risk in running repurposed exploit code. Expect crashes, resprings, and kernel panics. Success rate is around 70% — you may need to retry. Use at your own risk.
Supported: iPhone, iOS 18.4 – 18.6.2
Repurposed from an in-the-wild exploit chain. All original C2 communication removed.
Full source code on GitHub.

Takes up to 60 seconds. Do not switch apps or lock your device while running. If the page refreshes immediately after pressing Install, try again.

There is no elegant way to detect completion. After about a minute, try dragging a 5th icon into your dock. If it doesn't work, reboot and retry.
